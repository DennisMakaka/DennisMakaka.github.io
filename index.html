About SmartSpend

SmartSpend was inspired by my personal experience of struggling with financial management. I needed an easy and reliable way to track my spending, schedule payments, and generate detailed reports to see where my money was going. This project was developed over the course of a few months as part of my journey to create helpful financial tools for personal and professional use. As a Portfolio Project for Holberton School, SmartSpend is a testament to my growth as a developer.
